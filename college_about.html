College History

Velammal College of Engineering and Technology (VCET) was established in 2007 to provide quality technical education in South Tamil Nadu. Over the years the college expanded its departments, facilities, and industry partnerships—growing into a respected technical institution anchored in research, placements, and value-based education.

  • Established: 2007 (VCET).
  • Affiliation: Anna University (Autonomous since 2021 listed on public sources).
